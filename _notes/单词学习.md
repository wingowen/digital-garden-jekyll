### 01

There is considerable debate over how we should react if we detect a signal from an alien civilisation.

**debate over + 名词/从句** 是固定用法，其中 **over** 相当于 **about** 或 **concerning**，但语气更正式或强调争议性。类似的例子还有：
- There’s a heated debate over climate change policy.  
- They had an argument over who should pay the bill.
